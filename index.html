About Us

Emilia Banks Collection is a Nigerian-inspired fashion house celebrating the vibrant beauty of African heritage through Asoebi, bridal, and wedding guest dresses. Our designs fuse tradition with modern sophistication, crafted to empower every woman.

Mission: To empower women with style that honors tradition while embracing modern elegance.
